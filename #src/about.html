About ebssa

EBSSA was founded in 2014, as a provider of High level Tactical and Firearms training Courses. All EBSSA instructors are highly motivated and professional, drawn from various international military, special operations, law enforcement and commercial agencies.

These personnel have a wealth of exceptional worldwide experience and are renowned for dealing with high-risk situations and complex operations. Since the moment of foundation, we are offering both civilian and tactical tactical courses, providing special training for law enforcement and military units from different countries.

EBSSA provides the facilities and organize tactical courses in Serbia, Mexico, Jordan, Italy. We have a mobile training team and we are able to organize courses all over the World
