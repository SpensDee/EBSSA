About facility

26 hectares of training ground, including different types of shooting ranges, training zones for all types of military drills, different types of rifles and handguns, mobile training team that can organize a course on demand.

We are ready to all type of cooperation, if you have any question don’t hesitate to contact us.

The Close Protection Operative course will teach you how to manage security of a VIP, organize a security team and all the logistics of prevention.
